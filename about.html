About Shopee

Leading the Future of E-commerce

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Shopee has grown from a small startup to one of the region's leading e-commerce platforms.

We believe in making online shopping accessible, secure, and enjoyable for everyone. Our platform connects millions of buyers and sellers, creating opportunities and bringing convenience to people's lives.

What started as a simple idea has transformed into a comprehensive ecosystem that serves diverse communities across multiple countries.
