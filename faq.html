Account & Security

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping is available for select items and locations, with delivery in 1-2 business days. International shipping may take 7-14 business days depending on the destination.

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can track your order by entering this number in the "Track Order" section of your account or clicking the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email.

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and cash on delivery for eligible locations. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway.

Yes, all payment information is encrypted using industry-standard SSL technology. We never store your full credit card information on our servers. Our payment processing system complies with PCI DSS requirements for secure transactions.

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Products must be unused and in their original packaging. Some items, such as personalized products or intimate apparel, are not eligible for return. Please check the product page for specific return eligibility.

Once we receive your returned item, refunds are typically processed within 3-5 business days. The time it takes for the refund to appear in your account depends on your payment method and financial institution, usually 5-10 business days.

Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password. For security reasons, password reset links expire after 24 hours.

Log in to your account and go to "Account Settings" or "Profile." Here you can update your personal information, shipping addresses, and communication preferences. Some information may require verification for security purposes.
